<p><strong>The Bend Fixtures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>3-point Bend</li><li>4-point Bend</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bend fixtures are essential tools in testing materials, specifically assessing their ductility and flexural properties. The 3-point bend fixture involves applying force at a single central point between two supports, making it ideal for evaluating material strength at low deformation levels. Conversely, the 4-point bend fixture distributes load across two points, offering a more uniform stress profile, which helps analyze materials under higher stress levels. Both types cater to various applications across industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
