<p><p>The Near Infrared (NIR) analyzers market can be categorized into various types based on their technology. FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) analyzers utilize interferometers for high-resolution spectral analysis, making them suitable for complex samples. Other technologies include AOTF (Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter), known for rapid wavelength adjustment, and filter-based NIR analyzers, which use fixed filters for specific applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food quality control, and agricultural analysis, enhancing operational efficiency and precision.</p></p>
