<p><p>The industrial radiography market comprises two main types: digital and film-based systems. Digital radiography utilizes advanced detectors to capture images instantly, offering superior image quality, faster processing times, and enhanced data management. In contrast, film-based radiography relies on traditional film exposure and development, which can be more time-consuming and less efficient. While film-based methods are often cost-effective for simpler applications, the growing demand for rapid and precise inspections is driving a shift towards digital technologies in industrial settings.</p></p>
