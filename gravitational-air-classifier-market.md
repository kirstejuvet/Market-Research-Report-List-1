Wet classifiers utilize water to separate particles based on density and size, ideal for mineral and chemical processing. In contrast, dry classifiers rely on airflow and gravitational forces to separate materials without the use of liquids, making them suitable for applications in recycling and powder processing. Both types serve vital roles in enhancing material quality and efficiency in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
