<p><p>Tartan tracks, used primarily in athletic facilities, can be constructed from two main types of rubber: natural and synthetic. Natural rubber, derived from latex, offers excellent elasticity and shock absorption, providing a comfortable surface for runners. In contrast, synthetic rubber, typically made from polymers like styrene-butadiene, is more durable and resistant to weathering and UV damage. Both types serve specific needs, with natural rubber favored for performance and feel, while synthetic rubber is prized for longevity and maintenance practicality.</p></p>
