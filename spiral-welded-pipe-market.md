<p><strong>The Spiral Welded Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spiral Welded Pipe market encompasses three primary types: ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes, LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. ERW pipes are known for their efficiency and cost-effectiveness in lower diameters. LSAW pipes offer superior strength and are used for larger diameters and high-pressure applications. SSAW pipes, characterized by their spiral weld, are ideal for fluid transportation and structural purposes, providing flexibility and adaptability in various projects.</p></p>
