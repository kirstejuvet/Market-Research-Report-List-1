<p><ul><li>Single Component</li><li>Two Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone adhesives for the semiconductor market are categorized into two main types: single-component and two-component adhesives. Single-component adhesives are pre-cured and ready to use, offering convenience and stability, making them ideal for applications requiring quick curing and consistent performance. In contrast, two-component adhesives consist of separate resin and hardener components that must be mixed before use, providing enhanced adhesion and flexibility. This versatility makes them suitable for demanding environments in semiconductor manufacturing where precision and durability are crucial.</p></p>
