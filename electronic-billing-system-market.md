<p><strong>The Electronic Billing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Biller-direct System</li><li>Bank-aggregator System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic billing system market comprises two main types: the biller-direct system and the bank-aggregator system. In the biller-direct system, consumers receive bills directly from service providers, enabling immediate access and payment options through the provider's platform. Conversely, the bank-aggregator system consolidates bills from multiple service providers, allowing users to manage and pay their bills through their banking institution's interface. Both systems enhance convenience, streamline payment processes, and improve cash flow management for businesses and consumers alike.</p></p>
