<p><p>The torpedo market is categorized into guided and unguided torpedoes. Guided torpedoes utilize advanced targeting systems, allowing precise engagement of underwater and surface targets. They are equipped with guidance technologies such as sonar or GPS, enhancing accuracy and effectiveness.
