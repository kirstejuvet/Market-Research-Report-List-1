<p><strong>The Radiation Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Ionization Detectors</li><li>Semiconductor Detectors</li><li>Scintillation Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiation detector market comprises three main types: Gas Ionization Detectors, Semiconductor Detectors, and Scintillation Detectors. Gas Ionization Detectors measure ionizing radiation through ionization of gas molecules, favored for their simplicity and durability. Semiconductor Detectors utilize semiconductor materials to detect radiation, offering high resolution and compact size, ideal for precision applications. Scintillation Detectors convert radiation into visible light using scintillating materials, widely used for their sensitivity and speed in various fields, including medical imaging and nuclear safety.</p></p>
