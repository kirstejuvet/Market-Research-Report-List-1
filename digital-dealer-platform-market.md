<p><p>The Digital Dealer Platform market encompasses two main types: Single-Dealer Platforms (SDPs) and Multi-Dealer Platforms (MDPs). An SDP is operated by a single financial institution, providing its clients with exclusive access to the institution’s own products and services. In contrast, an MDP aggregates offerings from multiple dealers, allowing clients to compare and access a wider range of financial products from various sources. Both platforms enhance trading efficiency and transparency in the digital marketplace.</p></p>
