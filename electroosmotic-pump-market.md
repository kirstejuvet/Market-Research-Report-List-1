<p><p>The electroosmotic pump market features several distinctive types. Porous electroosmotic pumps leverage porous materials to facilitate fluid movement through channels, ensuring uniform flow rates. Planar shallow electroosmotic pumps are designed with a flat profile, enabling efficient integration into compact devices, often used in microfluidics applications. Each type addresses specific operational requirements in fluid transport and handling.</p></p>
