<p><ul><li>Resting ECG Devices</li><li>Stress ECG Devices</li><li>Holter Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECG telemetry devices encompass a range of equipment used for monitoring heart activity. Resting ECG devices capture the heart's electrical signals while the patient is at rest, providing a baseline assessment. Stress ECG devices monitor heart performance during physical activity or stress tests, helping to identify potential cardiovascular issues under exertion. Holter monitors are portable devices worn for continuous, long-term ECG recording, typically over 24-48 hours, allowing for comprehensive heart activity analysis in daily life.</p></p>
