<p><strong>The Exhaust Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Exhaust Fans</li><li>Inline Centrifugal Exhaust Fans</li><li>Radial Exhaust Fans</li><li>Tubeaxial and Vaneaxial Exhaust Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exhaust fans market encompasses various types designed for specific ventilation needs. Centrifugal exhaust fans generate high pressure and are ideal for large spaces. Inline centrifugal exhaust fans, compact and installed in duct systems, enhance air movement with minimal space usage. Radial exhaust fans are efficient for high-volume airflow in demanding applications. Tubeaxial fans are suitable for lower pressure systems with high flow requirements, while vaneaxial fans combine features of axial and centrifugal designs, offering versatile performance in diverse settings.</p></p>
