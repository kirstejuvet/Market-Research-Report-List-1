<p><p>The milk protein isolate market comprises two primary types: casein and whey. Casein, a slow-digesting protein, is often used for its gelling properties and is popular in cheese production and dietary supplements. Whey, on the other hand, is a fast-digesting protein derived during cheese making and is favored in sports nutrition for its rapid absorption and high amino acid content. Both types are used in various applications, including food products, health supplements, and functional foods, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
