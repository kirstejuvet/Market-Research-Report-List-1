<p><p>Thermal receipt printers are categorized into three market types based on connectivity: wireless, Bluetooth, and NFC. Wireless printers connect to devices via Wi-Fi, allowing for flexible usage across various locations without the need for cables. Bluetooth printers offer a short-range, low-energy connection, ideal for mobile applications and direct pairing with smartphones or tablets. NFC (Near Field Communication) printers enable quick, contactless transactions by simply tapping compatible devices, enhancing convenience and speed for retail and hospitality environments.</p></p>
