<p><p>The Linear Heat Sensor market is divided into two main types: digital and analog sensors. Digital linear heat sensors utilize microelectronics to provide precise temperature readings and often feature advanced capabilities such as connectivity and data logging. In contrast, analog linear heat sensors respond to temperature changes with a continuous electrical output, making them simpler and potentially more cost-effective for basic applications. Both types serve critical roles in fire detection and management systems, ensuring safety in various environments.</p></p>
