<p><ul><li>Pressure Measurement</li><li>Flow Measurement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipeline leak detectors are crucial for ensuring the integrity of pipeline systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: pressure measurement and flow measurement. Pressure measurement detectors monitor changes in pressure within the pipeline, signaling potential leaks when abnormalities occur. Flow measurement detectors track the volume of fluid moving through the pipeline; inconsistencies in flow rates can indicate leaks. Both technologies contribute to safety, environmental protection, and efficient operation of pipeline infrastructure by enabling timely detection and response to leaks.</p></p>
