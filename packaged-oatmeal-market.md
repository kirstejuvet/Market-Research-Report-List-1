<p><p>The packaged oatmeal market features various types catering to diverse consumer preferences. Whole oat groats are the least processed and retain the most nutrients. Steel cut oats are chopped groats, offering a chewy texture. Scottish oats are ground oats, creating a creamy consistency. Regular rolled oats are steamed and flattened, making them versatile for cooking. Quick rolled oats are thinner and cook faster, while instant oats are pre-cooked and ready in minutes. Other types may include flavored or specialty oats.</p></p>
