<p><p>The chalk marker market includes various types, primarily categorized by nib design: bullet nib and chisel nib. Bullet nib markers feature a rounded tip, providing a consistent line thickness, ideal for precise detailing or writing. In contrast, chisel nib markers have a slanted edge, allowing for versatility in line width, suitable for both bold strokes and fine details. These markers are popular for use on non-porous surfaces like glass, ceramics, and whiteboards, catering to artists, educators, and businesses alike.</p></p>
