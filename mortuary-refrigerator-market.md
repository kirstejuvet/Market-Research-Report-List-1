Temporary storage refrigerators are designed for short-term preservation of deceased bodies, maintaining suitable temperatures for immediate need and limited duration. In contrast, body freezers provide long-term storage solutions, operating at lower temperatures to ensure the preservation of bodies for extended periods. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in funeral homes, morgues, and medical facilities, ensuring dignified handling and preservation of remains.</p></p>
