<p><strong>The Metallurgical Coal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Coking Coals (HCC)</li><li>Medium Coking Coal</li><li>Semi-soft Coking Coal (SSCC)</li><li>Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallurgical coal market is divided into several categories based on coking properties. Hard Coking Coals (HCC) are the highest quality, ideal for steel production due to their strong coking ability. Medium Coking Coals are versatile, producing a good quality coke. Semi-soft Coking Coals (SSCC) offer a lower coking capacity but are still valuable for blending. Pulverized Coal Injection (PCI) Coal is used to supplement coke in blast furnaces, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs while supporting steelmaking processes.</p></p>
