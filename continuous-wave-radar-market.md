<p><strong>The Continuous Wave Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unmodulated Continuous Wave</li><li>Modulated Continuous Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous wave radar can be categorized into two main types: unmodulated and modulated continuous wave radar. Unmodulated continuous wave radar emits a constant frequency signal, primarily used for speed detection in applications like traffic monitoring. In contrast, modulated continuous wave radar varies its frequency or phase to provide distance measurements and enhanced target discrimination, commonly used in applications such as automotive radar and airborne surveillance. Each type serves distinct operational needs across various industries, driving the radar market's growth.</p></p>
