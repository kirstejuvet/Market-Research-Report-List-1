<p><strong>The Auto-Tool Changer (ATC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum Type Changers</li><li>Chain Type Changers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto-Tool Changer (ATC) market is vital for enhancing automation in machining and manufacturing processes by enabling quick tool exchanges. Drum type changers utilize a circular arrangement of tools, facilitating compact designs and efficient storage, suitable for smaller machines. In contrast, chain type changers employ a linked mechanism to hold tools, allowing for a larger selection of tools in a more linear format. Both types enhance productivity, reduce downtime, and improve production flexibility in various industrial applications.</p></p>
