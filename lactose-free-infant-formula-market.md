<p><p>The lactose-free infant formula market is primarily divided into two types: milk infant formula and non-milk infant formula. Milk infant formula is derived from cow's milk but is specially processed to remove lactose, making it suitable for lactose-intolerant infants. Non-milk infant formula, on the other hand, is made from alternative protein sources such as soy, goat's milk, or hydrolyzed proteins, catering to infants who may have more severe allergies or intolerances. Both types aim to provide essential nutrients for healthy growth.</p></p>
