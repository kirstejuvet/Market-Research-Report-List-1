<p><p>The active sonar market encompasses various types, notably Multi-Beam and Side Scan Sonar Systems. Multi-Beam Sonar Systems use multiple beams to create detailed underwater topographies, making them ideal for hydrographic surveys and seabed mapping. Side Scan Sonar Systems, on the other hand, employ a single beam to produce images of the seafloor, effectively identifying objects and structures. Both technologies are crucial in marine research, underwater exploration, and naval operations, providing critical data for a variety of applications.</p></p>
