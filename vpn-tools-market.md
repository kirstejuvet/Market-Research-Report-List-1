<p><p>The VPN tools market is primarily divided into two types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN enables individual users to securely connect to a private network from remote locations, ensuring data privacy and security. This is ideal for remote workers or traveling professionals. In contrast, Site-to-Site VPN connects entire networks, allowing secure communication between different office locations. This type is crucial for businesses with multiple branches, enabling seamless data transfer and unified communication across all sites.</p></p>
