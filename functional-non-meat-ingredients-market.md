<p><p>The functional non-meat ingredients market comprises various components used to enhance meat products. Binders, such as starches and proteins, help maintain texture and cohesion. Extenders increase volume and reduce costs, often using cereals or legumes. Fillers, like soy protein, add bulk without altering flavor significantly. Coloring and flavoring agents improve the appearance and taste of products. Salts and preservatives enhance shelf life and food safety, preventing spoilage and maintaining product quality. Collectively, these ingredients are crucial for improved meat formulations.</p></p>
