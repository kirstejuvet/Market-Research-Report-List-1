<p><p>The Real-time Motion Capture (Mocap) System market is divided into two primary types: Optical Systems and Non-optical Systems. Optical Systems utilize cameras and markers to track motion within a defined space, offering high precision and fidelity, making them ideal for film and gaming. Non-optical Systems encompass technologies like inertial sensors and magnetic tracking, providing greater flexibility and mobility. These systems are often used in applications like virtual reality and sports analysis, where precise environmental control may not be available.</p></p>
