<p><strong>The Insulin Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Pump</li><li>Patch Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The insulin pump market consists of two primary types: normal pumps and patch pumps. Normal pumps are traditional devices typically worn on a belt or clipped to clothing, delivering insulin through a catheter inserted under the skin. In contrast, patch pumps are a more discreet option that adheres directly to the skin, providing a wireless and tubeless insulin delivery system. Both types offer advanced features for managing diabetes, but they cater to different user preferences and lifestyles.</p></p>
