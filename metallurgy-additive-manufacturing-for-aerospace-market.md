<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metallurgy Additive Manufacturing for aerospace primarily involves two advanced techniques: Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses high-powered lasers to selectively melt and fuse metal powders, creating intricate components with high precision and strength. EBM, on the other hand, employs an electron beam in a vacuum to melt metal powders layer by layer, allowing for even greater control over material properties. Both methods enable lightweight structures and complex geometries essential for aerospace applications, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
