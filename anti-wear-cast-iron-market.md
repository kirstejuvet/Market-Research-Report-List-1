<p><p>The anti-wear cast iron market is primarily divided into two types: white cast iron and chilled cast iron. White cast iron is characterized by its hard, brittle structure, making it suitable for applications requiring high wear resistance, such as in grinding mills and pump components. In contrast, chilled cast iron features a hard surface due to rapid cooling during casting, enhancing its toughness and abrasion resistance. Both types are essential in industries like mining, construction, and manufacturing for their durability and performance.</p></p>
