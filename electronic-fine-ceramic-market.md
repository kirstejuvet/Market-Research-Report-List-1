<p><strong>The Electronic Fine Ceramic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electronic Structural Ceramics</li><li>Electronic Functional Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic fine ceramic market is divided into two primary types: electronic structural ceramics and electronic functional ceramics. Electronic structural ceramics are materials designed for mechanical stability and reliability in electronic components, often used in capacitors, insulators, and substrates. On the other hand, electronic functional ceramics are materials that exhibit specific electrical, magnetic, or optical properties, essential for applications such as piezoelectric sensors, capacitors, and semiconductor devices, enabling advanced functionality in electronic systems.</p></p>
