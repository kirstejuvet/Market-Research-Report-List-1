<p><strong>The Aerospace Tyre Pressure Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Type</li><li>Wired Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aerospace Tyre Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market comprises two main types: Wireless and Wired. Wireless TPMS utilizes sensors embedded within the tyres to transmit pressure data to a central system without physical connections, enhancing installation flexibility and reducing weight. In contrast, Wired TPMS relies on direct connections between sensors and the monitoring system, offering potentially more robust data transmission. Both types are essential for ensuring optimal tyre performance and safety in the aerospace sector, catering to different operational needs and aircraft designs.</p></p>
