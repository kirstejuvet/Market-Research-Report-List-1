<p><ul><li>Axial Flow</li><li>Radial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reaction steam turbine market is primarily categorized into axial flow and radial flow designs. Axial flow turbines utilize a design where the steam passes parallel to the turbine axis, providing higher efficiency and compactness, making them suitable for large power plants. In contrast, radial flow turbines direct steam at a right angle to the axis, offering robustness and simplicity, often favored in smaller, specialized applications. Each type serves unique operational requirements, impacting their deployment and market demand.</p></p>
