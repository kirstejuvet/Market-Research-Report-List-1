<p><p>The fiber optic probe market includes several types, primarily categorized into reflection probes, dip probes, and other specialized probes. Reflection probes are designed to measure light reflection from surfaces, making them ideal for non-destructive testing. Dip probes are utilized for fluid analysis, allowing for in-situ measurements of liquid samples. Other types encompass various designs tailored for specific applications, such as endoscopic procedures or environmental monitoring, enhancing diagnostic capabilities across multiple fields by providing accurate, real-time data.</p></p>
