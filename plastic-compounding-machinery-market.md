<p><p>The plastic compounding machinery market encompasses various types of equipment essential for processing polymers. Kneaders are designed for high-viscosity materials, providing effective blending and shearing. Mixers focus on uniform distribution of additives and fillers in plastics, ensuring consistent quality in the final products.</p></p>
