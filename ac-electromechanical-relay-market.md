<p><ul><li>PCB Mounting</li><li>DIN Rail Mounting</li><li>Panel-mount</li><li>Surface-mount</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC electromechanical relay market comprises several types based on mounting techniques. PCB mounting relays are designed for direct installation on printed circuit boards, offering compactness and ease of integration. DIN rail mounting relays are suited for organized electrical installations, providing stability and accessibility. Panel-mount relays are installed directly onto control panels, facilitating user interaction. Surface-mount relays are designed for surface soldering, optimizing space in compact electronics. Other market types include specialty relays for unique applications, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
